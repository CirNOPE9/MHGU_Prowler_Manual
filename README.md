# MHGU_Prowler_Manual
A manual implementation of MHGU's Prowler content for Archipelago. (Up to HR atm)

### Current (-ly planned) Locations:
- Hub and Village Prowler quests
### Current (-ly planned) Items:
- Palico Biases (with Charisma as the starting one)
- Skill Points
- Move Slots (so if you have 3 then you can only bring 3 moves to the hunt)
- Available Hub and Village stars (Village Stars for Village, Hunter Rank for Hub; starting from Village 2*)
- Armor and Weapon Rarities
- Palico Vouchers (how many palicoes you're allowed to bring to hunts)
- Power/Armorcharms and -talons (yes they do work as cats that's why they are items)
- Meownster Hunter Voucher (mcguffin for final quest)
### End Goal
- Finish Meownster Hunter (final HR Hub Prowler quest)

## Setup
Any MHGU (MHXX) savefile that's HR8+ and has caught up in Village as well
You may use any Palico that you have / can get your hands on
